Philosophy

NGSC is driven by three core pillars: Social, Sports and Leisure. The Social pillar is run by the full-fledge of sports facilities and buildings that integrate the members within a community of shared interest and common values. The Sports pillar is powered by providing unparalleled facilities to power the member’s physical and mental wellbeing. Whereas, the Leisure pillar is evident in the club’s wide-range of tranquil and dynamic recreational experiences.

Recreation is about activities and experiences which produce feelings of joy and satisfaction. They offer opportunities to express creativity as well as achieve and master new skills.

Innovation is key to NGSC’s philosophy, which boasts it as the number one hub with progressive, cutting-edge facilities that are regularly maintained and upgraded, as well as state-of-the-art programs and activities that cater to all ages, especially children.

Driven by a strong belief that kids shape future generations, we strive to stimulate their mental and physical growths by activating and boosting their young minds and bodies through an array of programs and activities. Accordingly, we encourage our children to power through and contribute as competitive individuals and champions for centuries to come.
